Bear Rally Key Mechanics

  • All three expedition skills of the rally leader activate
  • The entire rally's base Attack and Lethality are determined by the leader's stats. The leader's gear, research, and hero stars determine how hard the rally hits.
  • Only the top 4 riders by skill level contribute their "1st Expedition Skill." The leader should verify that riders' first heroes are set correctly. Riders' own gear, research, and attack panel are irrelevant — inside a rally, the leader's panel applies.
  • That's why Chenko's 1st expedition skill (+25% Lethality) should be maxed first
  • Gear only applies when you are the rally leader — your gear has no effect when joining someone else's rally
  • Sig Weapons give multiplicative bonuses, gear gives additive bonuses → Sig Weapons become more valuable late game
  • Don't send a single troop type. Damage does not scale linearly with troop count (diminishing returns). If 10,000 archers deal 1M damage, 40,000 archers (4×) deal only $\sqrt{4} = 2$× = 2M damage.
  • Base damage weight by troop type: Infantry ×$\frac{1}{3}$, Cavalry ×$1$, Archer ×$\frac{4}{3}$. This means archers generate the highest base damage per unit of troop capacity. → Max archers first, minimal infantry, cavalry fills the gap.

4. Pet Development

  • Gray Wolf : Construction speed boost — best early helper before City Hall 30. Breakthrough to max level; no need to invest refine resources.
  • Lynx : Provides extra stamina. Breakthrough to max level; no need to invest refine resources. Monthly-pass players rarely run short on stamina, so investing refines into Lynx is optional depending on your situation.
  • Bison : Instantly completes a gathering task. Breakthrough to max level to shorten cooldown; early refines to blue tier. Extremely useful during KvK / Alliance Showdown / Full Mobilization.
  • Cheetah : Provides food rations. Level up to unlock skills but don't invest refines too early.
  • Elk : Combat powerhouse pet — upgrade and refine when you have spare resources.
  • Lion : Randomly grants lord materials. Breakthrough to max to shorten cooldown; refine to blue tier.
  • Grizzly : Increases Attack. Combat powerhouse — breakthrough to max, refine to blue tier.
  • Great Bison : Increases squad capacity. Rally god-pet — must breakthrough to max!
  • Great Elk : Increases rally capacity.
  • Panther : Increases Lethality. Combat powerhouse — breakthrough to max, refine to blue tier.

5. Master Academy

A recently added system similar to pets, but all skills are passive.

  • Valora
    • Hunting Instinct (Innate): Increases damage dealt to the Giant Bear (self only). If you are the rally leader, only you get this buff — riders do not benefit. Still useful as a rider too.
    • Hunt's Dance: Increases rally capacity for Giant Bear hunts (must-have for rally leaders) — lets more alliance members join your rally for higher hunt scores.
    • Experience Inheritance: Bear hunt rewards grant additional equipment EXP parts.
    • Weapon Mastery: Bear hunt rewards grant additional Forge Hammers. (F2P must-max!)
    • Numerical Advantage: Increases your own march capacity for bear hunts. Useless if your alliance has a march cap restriction.
  • Pan
    • Resource Stockpile (Innate): Daily reset — every 120 min of accumulated gathering earns a bonus supply chest. Chests have a chance to contain Gold.
    • Falcon Scout: Daily reset — discover additional Intelligence events.
    • Meal Management: The 08:00 and 20:00 daily stamina refills grant more than 120 stamina.
    • Construction Plan: Reduces construction time.
    • Special Channel: Daily reset — extra Mystery Shop refreshes and additional Mystery Badges.
  • Roman
    • Born Fighter (Innate): Each arena battle has a chance to drop a Competition Star Chest. Chests have a chance to contain Forge Hammers.
    • Fearless Brave: Guards (small soldiers flanking heroes) gain extra Attack and HP.
    • Savor Victory: Daily and weekly arena settlement rewards grant extra Arena Coins.
    • March to Glory: Arena Shop offers discounts and more items to choose from.
    • Center Stage: Heroes in the arena gain increased Attack and HP.
Personally, most Master Badges go into Pan. The other two are only leveled to Acquaintance 3 (unlocking the 3rd skill) before circling back.
Pan's Meal Management — skip entirely if you're a monthly-pass player (you won't lack stamina); F2P players should invest heavily.
Falcon Scout — max it out: extra Intelligence mission score during KvK and Alliance Showdown, plus a steady trickle of Gold in downtime.
Special Channel — max it out: more chances to find free and 50%-off Sig Weapon shards and advanced pet refine items.

6. Resource Management

Building Priority

City Hall > Training Ground > Academy > Command Center > Others

  • Hospital, Kitchen, House → stop upgrading at level 10
  • Resource buildings → no need to upgrade
  • Goal: push City Hall to level 30 to unlock T10 troops
Tip: Activate the Gray Wolf skill (Construction Speed) and Decree skill (Accelerate Construction) first. If you also hold the Prime Minister title at the same time, then start the building upgrade — this can cut construction time by ~30%.

Research Priority

Command Tactics > Training Equipment > Build Speed > Research Speed > Gathering > Squad Expansion

Gear Upgrade Priority

Forge Hammer & Legendary Gear & Mithril investment order: Archer > Infantry > Cavalry

  1. Top priority Archer Lethality (Helmet / Boots) — maximizes your bear-hunt score and damage output, earning more Forge Hammers.
  2. Infantry HP (Chest / Gloves)
  3. Cavalry Lethality (Helmet / Boots)
Why infantry over cavalry: infantry tanks all incoming damage for the whole army — the longer infantry survives, the more damage archers and cavalry deal.

Gem Spending Priority

  1. 2nd Building Queue (permanent, worthwhile micro-spend ~$100)
  2. 5th Expedition Queue (permanent, most important micro-spend ~$100) — either purchase unlocks the Bank's Daily Savings.
  3. Hero Roulette — all remaining gems go here
  4. Do NOT spend on: speed-ups, temporary building queues, cosmetics

Daily Task Checklist

  • Claim stamina every 12 hours (08:00 and 20:00)
  • Complete daily quests
  • Wandering Shop: buy VIP EXP and discounted speed-ups
  • Mystery Shop (free items + discounts) and 50%-off Sig Weapon Shards
  • Arena Shop: redeem Legendary Hero Gear Custom Box
  • VIP Shop: redeem Lord Stamina (Drumstick) and Advanced Teleport
  • Alliance Shop: daily redeem discounted speed-ups and Advanced Teleport; weekly redeem Pet Breakthrough Material Custom Box
  • Supremacy Shop: Pet Medals, Universal Master Badge, Advanced Pet Refine
  • Holy Sword Shop: collect all three hero shards; temporary lord gear / gems when needed
  • KvK Shop: Gold, Yellow Fragments
  • Tide Shop: Advanced Refine, Pet Medals, temporary lord gear / gems
  • Challenge Trial Shop: Mithril, Gear Boxes, Yellow Fragments
  • Participate in alliance events, arena, dungeon trials (preset troops at 50:20:30)
  • Keep buildings and research running non-stop
  • Claim all basic event rewards
  • Weekly lord gear / lord gem Enhancement Material Exchange

9. Common Mistakes (Must Avoid)

  1. Spreading hero investment too thin — stick to 3 core heroes; max each one before moving to the next
  2. Evenly distributing skill points — concentrate on one skill branch
  3. Leaving builders idle — every idle second is wasted
  4. Skipping events — Hall of Governors and similar events have huge rewards; save speed-ups and resources for event periods
  5. Ignoring the Mystery Shop — multiple daily refreshes offer free items
  6. Not joining a strong alliance early — join an active alliance within the first 2 days
  7. Spending gems too early — gems are only for the Hero Roulette
  8. Using Emergency Mobilization and Rush simultaneously — they don't stack; using both at once wastes one
  9. Neglecting City Hall upgrades — City Hall level caps all other buildings
  10. Ignoring blue-tier heroes — blue heroes still have value for gathering
  11. Maxing old hero Sig Weapons over investing in new heroes — don't abandon already-invested heroes too quickly
  12. F2P tip: prioritize Lord Gear, Lord Gems, and Pet Development over hero Sig Weapons. New heroes release every ~2 months and their base stats will outclass older generations, leading to exhausting constant hero swaps.
  13. War Academy exchange — use only Coins to exchange for Gold Dust, never Gold. Gold is a critical upgrade resource; Coins are far less valuable to us.

11. Bundle Purchase Recommendations

  • First choice: Queue Bundle — one-time ~$100, unlocks the 5th expedition queue and the Bank's daily savings. Best value in the game.
  • Second choice: Super Monthly Pass — ~$330/month, unlocks the 6th queue, 14,000 diamonds, and 600 stamina drumsticks. Most importantly enables Auto-Hunt, freeing your hands during the last two days of Alliance Showdown.
  • Second choice (tied): Login Gift — ~$33/week, grants 10 Gold (≈$3.3/Gold), plus 2× yellow universal shards, lord materials, and various 1-hour speed-ups. All high-value items for KvK prep.

Purchasing both monthly bundles totals ~$462/month.
